Filet Mignon

Description

Indulge in the ultimate dining experience with our simple yet elegant filet mignon recipe. Tender and
succulent filet mignon steaks, seasoned with a perfect balance of salt and pepper, are seared to mouthwatering
perfection in a hot skillet. Finished with a luxurious blend of butter, garlic, and fragrant herbs,
each bite of these juicy steaks is a symphony of flavor. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or
simply treating yourself to a gourmet meal, our filet mignon recipe is sure to impress with its simplicity and exquisite taste.
Elevate your dining experience tonight with this classic favorite!

Ingredients

  • filet mignon steaks (6-8 oz)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Optional: minced garlic, fresh herbs like thyme or rosemary

Cooking Instructions

  1. Preparation: Remove the filet mignon steaks from the refrigerator and let them sit at room temperature for about 30 minutes to ensure even cooking.
    Season both sides of each steak generously with salt and pepper.
  2. Cooking:Heat a skillet (preferably cast iron) over medium-high heat until it's very hot. Add olive oil to the skillet and swirl to coat the bottom.
    Carefully place the seasoned filet mignon steaks in the hot skillet, ensuring they are not overcrowded. Cook the steaks for 3-4 minutes on each
    side for medium-rare doneness, or adjust the cooking time according to your preferred level of doneness.
  3. Finishing: During the last minute of cooking, add butter to the skillet along with optional minced garlic and fresh herbs.
    Use a spoon to baste the steaks with the melted butter and herbs, infusing them with extra flavor.
  4. Resting and Serving: Once cooked to your desired doneness, transfer the filet mignon steaks to a plate or cutting board and let them rest for 5 minutes.
    This allows the juices to redistribute, resulting in a juicier steak. Slice the filet mignon against the grain into thick slices and serve immediately.
