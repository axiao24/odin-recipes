Quesadilla

Description

Introducing our mouthwatering chicken quesadilla! Imagine tender grilled chicken, melted cheese,
and a hint of seasoning, all snugly wrapped in a warm tortilla and toasted until golden and crispy.
It's a flavor-packed delight that's perfect for any occasion. Treat yourself to this classic favorite today!

Ingredients

  • Flour tortillas
  • Cooked chicken (shredded or diced)
  • Shredded cheese (such as cheddar, Monterey Jack, or Mexican blend)
  • Diced onions
  • Diced bell peppers
  • Sliced jalape√±os
  • Salsa
  • Sour cream
  • Guacamole

Cooking Instructions

  1. Prepare Ingredients: Shred cooked chicken and shred cheese.
  2. Assemble Quesadilla: Place tortilla on a flat surface. Sprinkle cheese on one half, add chicken,
    then sprinkle more cheese.
  3. Fold Tortilla: Fold tortilla over the filling to create a half-moon shape.
  4. Cook Quesadilla: Heat skillet over medium heat. Cook quesadilla for 2-3 minutes on each side until
    golden and cheese melts.
  5. Serve: Cut into wedges and serve hot with salsa, sour cream, or guacamole.
